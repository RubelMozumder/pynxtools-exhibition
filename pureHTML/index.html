overview

NOMAD is the open research data management platform for materials science — turning uploaded files into structured, searchable, and citable scientific knowledge.

• Parse automatically — upload raw files from 50+ simulation codes and experimental instruments; NOMAD creates structured entries without manual conversion
• Search across datasets — filter by material, element, method, or any metadata quantity to find relevant data across the entire community
• Publish & cite — assign a DOI to your dataset in one click, making your data a citable first-class research output
• Stay FAIR — every entry is Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable by design, satisfying journal and funder mandates
• Extend with plugins — add parsers, schemas, ELNs, and workflows via the plugin system; deploy your own instance with NOMAD Oasis

explore pynxtools framework

A unified framework to convert, validate, and publish experimental data — from raw vendor files to FAIR NeXus/HDF5.

• 9 reader plugins — SPM, XPS, EM, APM, MPES, ellipsometry, XRD, Raman, and Igor Pro
• Validate — checks field completeness, units, and data types against FAIR NeXus standards
• Extend — reuse an existing NeXus datamodel or define a new one with NXDL
• NOMAD-native — semantic search, DOI, and API access without extra configuration
